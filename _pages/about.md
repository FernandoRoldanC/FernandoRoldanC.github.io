Currently, I am a INRIA postdoctoral researcher in [CVN](https://cvn.centralesupelec.fr/) at Centralesupélec. I received my PhD. (2022) from Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, Valparaíso, Chile.

My research area is splitting algorithms for solving monotone inclusions with applications on convex optimization problems and image processing.
